Welcome to the COVID-19 Deaths Dashboard!

Hover over any state to see details regarding deaths and population!
The state with the highest death ratio will be the brightest red.

Tips: Above the left container, you will find buttons to help you navigate through different slides. Each of these slides will present and filter data for a specific underlying cause of death. Once you reach slide 5 you will be able to switch to Explore Mode by clicking Explore Timeline.

Explore Mode: In explore mode you will find a timeline slider that will allow you to filter by any month between the years of 2020-2021. You can also use this filter in combination with the underlying diseases by going back to different slides.
